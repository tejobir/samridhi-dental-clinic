i

Check-ups & Cleaning

A relaxed, unhurried examination with scaling and polishing that leaves your mouth feeling genuinely fresh. Small problems get caught while they are still small, which is exactly how we keep treatment conservative.

  • — Full oral health review with digital X-rays when needed
  • — Gentle ultrasonic scaling and polish
  • — A written plan only if something actually needs doing
ii

Tooth-Coloured Fillings

Composite fillings matched to your natural tooth shade, bonded with minimal drilling. Nobody should be able to tell you ever had a cavity, including you.

  • — Shade-matched to blend invisibly
  • — Preserves more of your natural tooth than metal fillings
  • — Usually done in a single, comfortable sitting
iii

Painless Root Canal

The treatment everyone dreads is the one our patients talk about most, because it genuinely doesn't hurt. Modern anaesthesia and a patient pace save a badly infected tooth instead of pulling it.

  • — Single-sitting RCT for most cases
  • — Saves your natural tooth from extraction
  • — Immediate relief from throbbing pain
iv

Crowns & Bridges

Strong, lifelike ceramic caps that protect a weakened tooth, and bridges that close a gap without surgery. Shade-matched against your own teeth so the result simply looks like you.

  • — Metal-free ceramic options available
  • — Precise shade matching for a natural look
  • — Protects root-canal-treated and cracked teeth
v

Braces & Clear Aligners

Dr. Chadha's fellowship in functional orthodontics means your teeth are straightened with your bite and jaw in mind, not just for the photograph. Near-invisible aligners fit working life; classic braces remain the workhorse.

  • — Clear aligners that most people never notice
  • — Functional orthodontics, not just cosmetic alignment
  • — Plans for teens and adults alike

vi

Dental Implants

The closest thing to growing a new tooth: a titanium root with a ceramic crown that looks, feels and chews like your own. A permanent answer to a missing tooth, without grinding down its neighbours.

  • — Permanent, natural-feeling replacement
  • — No damage to adjacent healthy teeth
  • — Eat everything you used to, with confidence

vii

Teeth Whitening

Dentist-supervised whitening that brightens safely in a single visit, without the sensitivity roulette of over-the-counter kits. We check your enamel first, then lift years of tea, coffee and time.

  • — Visible results in one sitting
  • — Enamel-safe, sensitivity-managed protocol
  • — Honest advice on what shade suits your smile
viii

Kids Dentistry

First visits designed to be fun, so children grow up seeing the dentist as a friend rather than a fear. Gentle cleanings, cavity care and honest guidance for parents on habits that matter.

  • — Fear-free first visits at your child's pace
  • — Fluoride and sealants to prevent cavities early
  • — Guidance on thumb-sucking, bottle habits and brushing

Wedding Smile Package

Smile design, whitening, veneers, aligners, tooth jewellery and Skyce dental diamonds, timed around your wedding date so every photograph catches you at your brightest. Packages for brides, grooms and the whole family.

  • — Book 4–6 weeks before the wedding for best results
  • — Bride, groom and family packages
