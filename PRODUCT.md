# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Families and working professionals in Kharghar / Navi Mumbai (Sectors 18–21 and surrounds) searching for a trustworthy local dentist, usually on a phone, often when something already hurts or before booking a long-postponed check-up. They are comparing a few nearby clinics and deciding who feels competent, gentle, and honest about cost. The secondary "user" is Dr. Puja Chadha herself: this site is a sales demo built to win her as a client.

## Product Purpose
A single-page marketing site for Samridhi Dental Super Speciality Clinic that turns a local search into a booked appointment. It must make Dr. Puja Chadha (BDS, FFO, DMLS) look credible and warm, explain her conservative, tooth-preserving approach, list treatments plainly, and put "Book on WhatsApp" / "Call" one tap away. Success = the doctor is impressed enough to buy it, and patients can book without friction.

## Brand Personality
Warm, calm, precise. A clinic that feels like unhurried personal care, not an assembly line. Voice is reassuring and plain-spoken: explains, never upsells; promises gentleness and honest pricing. Emotional goal: relief and trust ("I'm in careful hands").

## Anti-references
- Cold corporate hospital-blue medical sites with stock handshake photos.
- Loud discount-dentistry pages screaming "50% OFF" with clip-art teeth.
- Generic AI landing pages: cream background + one accent + tiny tracked eyebrows on every section + identical icon cards.
- Over-clinical galleries of surgery/blood that scare nervous patients.

## Design Principles
1. **Gentle over clinical.** Every choice should lower a nervous patient's heart rate, not raise it. Warmth carries the brand.
2. **One tap to booked.** Call and WhatsApp are always reachable; the path to an appointment is never more than a thumb away.
3. **Honest, specific care.** Real treatments, real hours, real location, conservative philosophy. No buzzwords, no inflated claims.
4. **Local and human.** This is a neighbourhood clinic run by one named doctor, not a faceless chain. Lead with the person and the place.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA: body text ≥4.5:1 on its background, large text ≥3:1, visible focus rings, full keyboard operability, semantic landmarks, `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured. Tap targets ≥44px for the mobile-first audience. Bilingual nod (clinic name appears in Devanagari) without forcing a full translation.
