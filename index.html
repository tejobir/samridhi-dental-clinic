Questions patients ask us most.

Will the treatment hurt?

For the vast majority of treatments, no. We use effective local anaesthesia and a slow, gentle technique, and most patients are surprised by how comfortable even root canals feel. If you are anxious, tell us, and we will go entirely at your pace.

How much will my visit cost?

You will always know the cost before any treatment begins. After your examination, Dr. Chadha shares a clear, conservative plan and a written estimate, so there are no surprises on the chair. EMI options are available for larger treatments.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. Walk-ins are welcome during clinic hours, but a quick WhatsApp message or call to book a slot means little to no waiting. For dental emergencies and pain, call us and we will fit you in as soon as possible.

Is the clinic good for children and nervous patients?

Very much so. We make first visits friendly and fear-free for kids, and adults who dread the dentist tell us this is the first clinic where they finally felt relaxed. Unhurried, gentle care is the whole point of how we work.
