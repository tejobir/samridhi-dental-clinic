Why choose clear aligners

  • —Nearly invisible appearance. Most people will not notice you are wearing them at all.
  • —Comfortable, custom-fit design. Smooth trays made to fit your teeth precisely.
  • —Removable for eating and brushing. No food restrictions, no awkward cleaning around wires.
  • —Easy to maintain oral hygiene. Brush and floss normally, just as you always have.
  • —Digitally planned treatment. A 3D plan means predictable, visible progress.

Is this treatment right for you?

Clear aligners can help correct:

  • —Crowded teeth
  • —Gaps between teeth
  • —Mild to moderate crooked teeth
  • —Minor bite issues

Not sure if you are a candidate? A quick consultation and 3D scan will tell you clearly, with no obligation.
