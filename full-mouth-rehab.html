Who full mouth rehab helps

  • —Several broken, worn down or heavily decayed teeth
  • —Multiple missing teeth, or old failing crowns and bridges
  • —A collapsed or painful bite, or difficulty chewing
  • —Years of postponed dental care you want to finally resolve

What it can combine

  • —Dental implants to replace missing teeth permanently
  • —Crowns & bridges to rebuild and protect weakened teeth
  • —Root canal treatment to save teeth worth keeping
  • —Gum care & orthodontics for a healthy, balanced foundation
